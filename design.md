# Viva Driving School — Design Guide

## Brand
- Name: Viva Driving School / Վիվա Վարելու Դպրոց
- Vibe: Professional SaaS, dark hero sections, clean white content areas, trustworthy

## Colors
- Primary: #2563EB (blue)
- Primary Dark: #1D4ED8
- Secondary: #1E293B (dark slate)
- Success: #22C55E
- Warning: #F59E0B
- Danger: #EF4444
- Background: #F8FAFC
- Dark Hero: #0F172A (near black)
- Card: #FFFFFF
- Border: #E2E8F0

## Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- H1: 3xl–5xl, font-bold
- H2: 2xl–3xl, font-semibold
- Body: base, font-normal
- Small: sm, text-slate-500

## Spacing
- 8px grid: use multiples of 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 12, 16, 20, 24

## Components Style
- Buttons: rounded-lg, px-6 py-3, primary = blue bg, secondary = outline
- Cards: rounded-xl, shadow-sm, white bg, p-6
- Inputs: rounded-lg, border-slate-200, focus:ring-blue
- Navbar: white bg with shadow on scroll, sticky top
- Hero: dark bg (#0F172A), white text, blue CTA
- Sections: alternating white / slate-50 bg

## Language
- EN/HY toggle in navbar top-right
- All UI text has both Armenian and English versions
